# VYN IQ – Android App Mock-Up (Full Visual Layout)

Concept: Material You + strategy game hybrid for a business-building simulator. Colors lean into matte blacks, soft sand gradients, neon highlights, and gold accents to keep the "old money" vibe while still feeling futuristic.

## 1. Android Home Screen (Empire Map View)
- **Top Bar:** Material You pill cards for Empire Score, Total Net Worth, Gems/Boosts, AI Insights (glowing brain icon), and Notifications badge.
- **Map Area:** Soft desert/field base map with interactive business buildings (tap to open each dashboard). Floating tooltips show profit today and stock warnings.
- **Right Action Column:** Vertical rail with AI Brain, Idea Forge, Pipeline, Staff, Events, and Alerts buttons (glassmorphic chips with neon edges).
- **Left Action Column:** Tasks, Daily Lab, Income Lab (25K/day), Journal quick buttons in frosted cards.
- **Bottom Navigation:** Rounded Material You bar with Businesses, Finance, Warehouse, and HQ tabs.

## 2. AI Brain Panel (Bottom Sheet)
- Slides up as a dark glass bottom drawer with neon glows.
- Sections: AI Insights Today, Performance Drops, Opportunities, Risk Alerts, and AI Agent Chat (CFO/COO/Legal/Strategy). Quick-reply chips and timeline bubbles.

## 3. Idea Forge (Dialog + Cards)
- Full-width dialog with matte black background and gold borders.
- Top input field for prompts; AI output as stacked scrollable cards: Business Name Suggestions, Model, Market Snapshot, 30/60/90 Plan, Cost Estimate.
- CTA: **Move to Pipeline →** anchored at bottom.

## 4. Pipeline Board (Horizontal Scroll)
- Mobile-friendly kanban with columns: Brainstorm, Validation, MVP, Launch, Growth, Profit Monitoring.
- Cards include title, business tag, progress bar; drag horizontally to change stage.

## 5. Task Manager (List + Quest UI)
- Sections for AI Tasks, Business Tasks, Income Tasks, Personal Tasks.
- Swipe-left-to-complete animation with green/gold glow. Progress rings beside each category.

## 6. Daily Lab (24-Hour Experiment Panel)
- Gamified challenge card: experiment title, countdown timer, steps, expected profit, success/fail tracking, and AI Debrief link.

## 7. Daily Income Lab (25K/day Progress)
- Gold progress bar toward 25,000 TZS target. Shows current progress and AI-suggested actions.

## 8. Business Dashboard (Analytics UI)
- Tabs: Overview, KPIs, Staff, Sales/Orders, AI Suggestions.
- Material You cards hosting line charts, bar charts, and highlight chips for surges/drops.

## 9. Finance Brain (Manager View)
- Cashflow line chart, business income breakdown, expenses list, debt countdown, rent reminder, and offshore plan progress card.

## 10. Staff Management (Card Deck)
- Employee cards with avatar, role, performance meter, assigned tasks, and AI delegation suggestions. Swipe to reassign.

## 11. Journal (Minimal Diary)
- Daily entry field, voice capture button, AI summary card, and timeline of past entries.

## 12. Achievements (Unlock Grid)
- Game-style grid for streaks, XP bar, levels, daily trophies, and milestones. Glow when unlocked.

## 13. HQ (Upgrade Screen)
- Strategy-game style HQ panel showing current level, requirements, benefits, and a prominent Upgrade button with timer.

## 14. Warehouse (Data Storage)
- File cabinet layout for exports, documents, files, and analytics snapshots. Quick filters and search chip row.
